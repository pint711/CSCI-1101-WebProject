Slam, or by its full name slamming brutal death metal, is an extreme subgenre of metal. It is a microgenre as it is a subgenre of brutal death, which iteslf is a subgenre of death metal, which is also a subgenre of heavy metal.

Characteristics

  • Guitars
    • Few, if any, guitar solos
    • Usually don't go faster than eighth notes
  • Drums
    • Blast Beats
    • Fast kick drums
  • Vocals
    • Unintelligble lyrics, if there are lyrics
    • Gutturals
    • Sound like a cricket, pig, a bullfrog, etc.

** Loud **
I recommend you lower your volume if you want to listen to the video examples.
 Harsh noise is an experimental subgenre of electronic music. It lacks rythm, melody, or really anything else that makes a song "a song".

Characteristics

  • Lack of traditional song structure
    • No melody or actual structure
  • Harshness
    • Loud and abrasive
  • Performative
